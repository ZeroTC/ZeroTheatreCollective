ALBY GRACE

Alby T Grace is an actor, writer and director. He played the Devil in Let's Be Devils and it was borderline inappropriate for a high school. He was also Demetrius in a version of Midsummer Night's Dream that got half a star.

EDDIE MORRISON

Eddie is an actor and has been on stage a lot and also a bit on the two types of screen (small, silver). He's even sometimes on the elusive third type of screen (computer, small computer (phone)). He plays a lot of racists and paedophiles, but isn't either of those things.

PETER CORTISSOS

Peter Cortissos is an actor and comedian. His best acting gig was when he looked after a fiberglass whale, covered in sexual-style lubricant, and lied to the public about its origins. The whale was covered in sexual-style lubricant, not Peter.

DEE EASTON

Dee Easton is a dramaturg, designer and actor. She once played a nun who was tied on a stake upside down, and another character urinated on her (it was fake urine). She dissociates when she thinks about this. She specialises in characters named 'Kitty' or 'Kat' or 'Bubbles.' She recently learned how to make tepache.
